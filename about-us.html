Macrotrees

An ISO 9001-2015 Company, is a pioneer manufacturer and exporter of high-quality natural coir products, established on 1/01/2019. The Company is educately backed-up by a team of efficient staffs, Managing Director who is adequately backed-up by a team of efficient staff/work force having an impeccable track record of immense experience and efficiency in the industry. During a very short span of time, macro leafs has been emerged as one of the leading and largest manufacturers and exporters of premium quality coir products. Our team constantly monitors the changing market patterns, and work closely with customers to understand their preferences.
